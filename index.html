What's all this then?

You're looking at - and hopefully listening to - a WebAssembly port of my new virtual instrument synth, Chonker. It's inspired and influenced by analogue classics, and named in honour of my cat and rubberduck-debugging-assistant.

It's a fully-featured VA synth with a production-grade audio engine. It'll connect to your default MIDI input device via WebMIDI - try it and let me know how you get on.

  • Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge. (Sorry, no Safari due to its lack of WebMIDI).
  • Supported CPUs: Anything capable of running WASM SIMD - Apple Silicon, Intel/AMD x64, ARMv8.
  • Audio engine: 4x oversampled, fully SIMD vectorised.
  • Oscillators: Standard, supersaw, SUB and various other goodies. Aliasing-free CLIO waveforms.
  • Filters: 0DF circuit modeled. "American", "Japan", SVFs... various circuits and response curves.
  • Effects: Multimode polyphonic FX and master effects chain.

The full plug-in will be available to buy in 2026. If you're interested in licensing the engine for your desktop, mobile or Web based project, get in touch.
